$E= \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}}\frac{Q}{r^{2}}$

$E= \frac{\sigma R^{2}}{\epsilon r^{2} }$
If P point is Inside the circle (r<R)
$E=0$
If P point is on the surface (r=R)
